About

A three-day scientific and technological exhibition called "Sasthrayaan 2023" is planned to be held from January 23 to 25, 2023, in order to make the vast knowledge accumulated by Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT) through its Research and Advanced Learning Activities more accessible to the general public. CUSAT Main Campus, Kalamassery, Cochin, will be the venue for the event. Exhibition, Science Conference, Discussions, Project Demonstrations and associated display, etc will be held spread across the seminar complex and various other departments. Industrialists, Businessmen, Politicians, Commoners, NGOs, Resident Associations, Students from Schools and Colleges, and all interested people can attend the event at Zero Registration Fees.
